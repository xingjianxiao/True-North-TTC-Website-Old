Our Club

    True North TTC is a community with players of all skills and ages. Our club is one of the national high performance clubs of Canada, home to countless national and international athletes. This includes U19 boys and girls national team athletes, as well as more experienced national team athletes for the men’s and women’s who have participated at the Tokyo 2020 olympics!
