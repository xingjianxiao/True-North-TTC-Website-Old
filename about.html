About Us

    True North TTC was a club founded by Hongtao Chen and Eugene Wang in 2018 to help others learn and perfect their table tennis skills. With their expert coaching, many athletes have reached new grounds in their table tennis journey and discovered new and exciting experiences. Now located in Scarborough, Ontario, we have over 50 members. Join us today!

Hongtao Chen (left), Eugene Wang (right)

Our Mission

    Our aim is to raise awareness about the sport of table tennis as it is one of the lesser known sports. We hope to spread the joy of the sport and help as many people as possible to discover table tennis! Table tennis is a sport of many benefits including: increased alertness and concentration, improved hand-eye coordination, and stimulated brain function.
